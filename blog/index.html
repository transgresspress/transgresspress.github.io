Manning Up Reviewed on Lamba Literary

Posted on July 31, 2014 • Blog

Lamba Literary has published a review of Manning Up: Transsexual Men on Finding Family, Brotherhood, and Themselves on their website!

This anthology is not just for trans men, but will resonate for many in the LGBT community as our coming out, growing up, and self-exploration changes continually as the world around us changes. This is an attractive collection that would be a valuable addition to writers, students and teachers.
