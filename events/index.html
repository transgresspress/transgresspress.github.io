Somerville, MA: Manning Up Book Launch

June 24, 2014 from 7:00-9:00pm EDT

We will be having a Manning Up Book Launch on Tuesday, June 24, 2014 from 7:00-9:00pm EDT in Somerville, MA at the Bloc 11 Cafe.

Join three trans writers – Mitch Kellaway, Nathan Ezekiel, and Justin Cascio – as they read their essays from an upcoming anthology on trans manhood and family: Manning Up: Transsexual Men on Finding Brotherhood, Family & Themselves”! After the reading, they will open up to questions and be available to sign book copies.

Light refreshments and copies of Manning Up will be available. The cafe will remain open if you want to purchase coffee or sandwiches.

More info about the book: http://www.manningupbook.com
